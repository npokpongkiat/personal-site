About

Hi, I’m Nathan. I’m a product designer based in Los Angeles, CA. I use my empathy and creativity to design efficient products for innovative companies. With a background in the sciences combined with an inquisitive mind, I ask the right questions to deliver effective solutions.

I draw from various experiences to fuel my designer chops. My background in testing hypotheses parallel the iterative and scientific nature of user experience design. With a few years of graphic design experience under my belt, I’ve gained a strong understanding of visual space and its essence in crafting a polished user interface. As a current creative lead for a small startup, I’ve been fortunate to tackle many diverse projects. These experiences altogether strengthen my work and who I am as a product designer.

When I’m not designing, I enjoy exploring music, discovering interesting digital creations online, and learning about new things on YouTube. My current obsession is motion graphics.
